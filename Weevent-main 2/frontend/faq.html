Weevents (wee events) is a platform designed to showcase points of interest and events happening near you. Whether you're looking for music festivals, art exhibits, or family-friendly activities, Weevents brings everything together on one easy-to-navigate website.

+

Weevents utilizes several APIs with geolocation to suggest the most relevant events based on your current location or preferences. Through our interactive dynamic map, you can explore events around your city or town, access hyperlinks for more information, and view public transport options to get there.

+

We curate a wide variety of events, including concerts, sports events, art exhibitions, family-friendly activities, and more. Event organizers can post and promote their events, allowing you to easily discover new things happening around you.

+

Weevents features an AI assistant powered by Llama 3.2, which can answer basic questions and provide personalized recommendations based on your preferences. Whether you are wondering what is happening this weekend or need event suggestions, the AI assistant is here to help.
