Your Questions, Answered

At Weevents, we aim to help you discover the best events happening around you, tailored to your interests and location.
Below are answers to some common questions about our platform and how it works.

Weevents (wee events) is a platform designed to showcase points of interest and events happening near you. Whether you're looking for music festivals, art exhibits, or family-friendly activities, Weevents brings everything together on one easy-to-navigate website and web app.

Weevents utilizes several APIs with geolocation to suggest the most relevant events based on your current location or preferences. Through our interactive dynamic map, you can explore events around your city or town, access hyperlinks for more information, and view public transport options to get there.

We curate a wide variety of events, including concerts, sports events, art exhibitions, family-friendly activities, and more. Event organizers can post and promote their events, allowing you to easily discover new things happening around you.

Absolutely! With Weevents, you can follow, save, share, and even like events you’re interested in. This makes it easier for you to keep track of exciting happenings and share them with friends.

If you’re an event organizer, you can post and advertise your events on Weevents. Our platform makes it simple to reach your target audience, allowing users to find your event easily via the dynamic map and search filters.

Weevents features an AI assistant powered by Llama 3.2, which can answer basic questions and provide personalized recommendations based on your preferences. Whether you’re wondering what’s happening this weekend or need event suggestions, the AI assistant is here to help.

Weevents is built using modern web technologies, including HTML5, JavaScript, and MySQL, with several API integrations for event data and geolocation services. Our platform’s backend will be powered by a robust framework (yet to be determined), ensuring a seamless experience.

Weevents integrates public transport options into the platform, allowing you to easily plan your journey to events. You’ll see nearby bus stops, train stations, and suggested routes when exploring events on the dynamic map.
